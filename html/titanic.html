Text look strange to you?

We have scanned the original newspaper pages using high quality scanners and an optical character recognition (OCR) process which creates the electronic text. Both these processes produce the most accurate results possible; however, it is inevitable that some errors slip through due to the age and condition of some of the original pages

Can I correct this text?

If you have noticed any errors in the text, select the article to edit to make any necessary corrections. We encourage all our users to do this to maintain the highest possible level of accuracy. When you make corrections, you are helping future users to get the most out of these pages. When you submit a correction to the OCR text, it will be available to view for all users within a few hours, but it may take up to 24 hours to be searchable on the site.

Am I seeing the original version?

You are shown the most recent corrected version of the OCR text. If no corrections have been made, you are shown the original OCR.
